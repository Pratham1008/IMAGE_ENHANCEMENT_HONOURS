About Us

LuxEnhance is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of image processing. We leverage Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and the Charbonnier Loss function to restore details in photos that were previously thought to be lost to the dark.

Our project focuses on balancing brightness, contrast, and noise reduction to provide a professional-grade enhancement experience for everyone.
